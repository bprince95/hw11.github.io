Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

This is the graph for City Latitude vs. Wind Speed. As you can see, the data for this scatter plot is very varied. They heaviest amount of dots accumulates at around 5 miles per hour and ranges from around -40 latitude to 70 latitude. What I got from this graph is that the highest wind speeds happen farther away from the equator. The highest wind speeds are at 35 miles per hour. It appears that the lowest are at zero although I have a hard time believing that anywhere has zero wind speed.
