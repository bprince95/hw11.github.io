Summary: Latitude vs. Temperature

This is the graph for City Latitude vs. Temperature. We can very clearly see the graph and the temperature change based on the latitude of the earth. The highest temperature is around 115 degrees farenheit at the latitude -20. At the equator the temperature looks to be between 70-90 degrees. Past that you can see a sharp decrease in the temperature based on the latitude. As you go north, the temperature gets colder.
